Jovany's Favorite Cybersecurity Practices

Here are some of the top security practices I believe every user should follow on a daily basis.

  1. Don't think you're not a target

    2. Everyone is at risk! Cybersecurity is everyone's responsibility.

    Protect yourself and others by following the following tips.

  2. Avoid getting Phished (emails, phone calls)

    3. Be aware of social engineering ploys by cyber-criminals to trick you into giving personal information.

  3. Maintain all software up-to-date

    4. Enable automatic updates on your OS

    Update all plugins and browser on a regular basis. Whether that be through automatic updates or manual.

  4. Maintain good password management

    5. Use a secure password manager to maintain strong unique passwords for all your accounts.

  5. Don't leave devices unattended. If you do... lock them!

    6. If you need to leave a device unattendad make sure its locked via software or encryption.

    This is even critical in an office setting! You never know who are your friends or enemies.
